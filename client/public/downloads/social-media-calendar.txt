Created by Lisgraphix | lisgraphix17@gmail.com
For Ghanaian businesses on Instagram, Facebook & TikTok

HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR
1. Replace [Business] with your business name
2. Replace [Service] with your main service
3. Take your own photos/videos for each post
4. Post at peak times: 7-9am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm (Ghana time)
5. Always add location tag: Accra, East Legon, etc.
