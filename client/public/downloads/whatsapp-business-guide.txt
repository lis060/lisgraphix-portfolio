WHATSAPP BUSINESS SETUP GUIDE
==============================
Created by Lisgraphix | lisgraphix17@gmail.com
Complete setup for Ghanaian business owners
==============================

WHY WHATSAPP BUSINESS?
- 95%+ of Ghanaians use WhatsApp daily
- Customers prefer to chat before buying
- Automated replies = leads even when you sleep
- Free to use, professional appearance

==============================
STEP 1 — DOWNLOAD THE APP
==============================
1. Go to Play Store or App Store
2. Search "WhatsApp Business" (green icon with B)
3. Download and install
4. Register with your BUSINESS phone number
   (different from your personal WhatsApp number)

==============================
STEP 2 — SET UP YOUR BUSINESS PROFILE
==============================
Open WhatsApp Business → Settings → Business Profile

Fill in ALL of these:
[ ] Business Name (your real business name)
[ ] Business Category (choose closest match)
[ ] Description (what you do, in 2-3 sentences)
[ ] Address (your location in Ghana)
[ ] Business Hours (Mon-Fri 8am-6pm etc.)
[ ] Website (your website URL)
[ ] Email address

TIP: A complete profile = more trust = more sales

==============================
STEP 3 — SET UP AUTOMATED REPLIES
==============================

A) GREETING MESSAGE (sent when someone messages first)
Settings → Business Tools → Greeting Message → Turn ON

Example greeting:
"Hello! Welcome to [Your Business Name]. 
Thank you for reaching out!

We offer:
✓ [Service 1]
✓ [Service 2]  
✓ [Service 3]

How can we help you today? 😊"

---

B) AWAY MESSAGE (sent when you're offline)
Settings → Business Tools → Away Message → Turn ON
Set hours: outside your business hours

Example away message:
"Hi! We're currently closed but we'll reply first thing 
in the morning.

Our hours: Mon-Sat, 8am-7pm

For urgent matters, call: +233 XX XXX XXXX

We look forward to serving you! 🙏"

==============================
STEP 4 — CREATE QUICK REPLIES
==============================
Settings → Business Tools → Quick Replies

These are shortcuts for messages you send often.

Shortcut: /price
Message: 
"Our pricing:
• Service 1 — GHS XX
• Service 2 — GHS XX
• Service 3 — GHS XX

Payment: Mobile Money accepted ✓
To book: Just confirm your preferred date and time!"

---

Shortcut: /location
Message:
"We are located at:
📍 [Your Address], [Area], Accra

Landmark: Near [Landmark]

Google Maps: [paste your Google Maps link]

We're open: [Your hours]"

---

Shortcut: /booking
Message:
"To book an appointment, please share:
1. Your name
2. Service needed
3. Preferred date
4. Preferred time

We'll confirm within 30 minutes! ✅"

==============================
STEP 5 — SET UP YOUR CATALOGUE
==============================
Settings → Business Tools → Catalogue → Add Product

For each product/service add:
- Name
- Price (in GHS)
- Description
- Photo (use a clean, bright image)

This turns your WhatsApp into a mini shop!

==============================
STEP 7 — WHATSAPP STATUS STRATEGY
==============================
Post to WhatsApp Status EVERY DAY:
- Monday: Motivational quote + your service
- Tuesday: Before & after / client result photo  
- Wednesday: A tip related to your industry
- Thursday: Product/service highlight
- Friday: Weekend promo or offer
- Saturday: Client testimonial
- Sunday: Behind the scenes / personal story

Views on status = free marketing to your contacts!

==============================
STEP 8 — WHATSAPP BROADCAST LISTS
==============================
Send one message to many clients at once.
(Unlike groups — each person receives it privately)

How to use:
1. Contacts → New Broadcast
2. Add your clients (they must have your number saved)
3. Type message → Send

Use for: Promotions, new products, announcements

==============================
PRO TIPS
==============================
✓ Always reply within 1 hour during business hours
✓ Use voice notes for complex explanations
✓ Save client names in your phone contacts
✓ Never spam — only message clients who know you
✓ Always end conversations professionally
