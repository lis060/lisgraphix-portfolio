SECTION 1 — COLOR PSYCHOLOGY FOR GHANAIAN MARKET
==========================================

GOLD / YELLOW (#c9a96e, #FFD700)
- Meaning: Wealth, premium, royalty, success
- Best for: Luxury brands, high-end services, jewellery
- Ghanaian context: Gold is deeply cultural — kente, Ashanti royalty
- Use for: Accents, CTAs, logos for premium brands

BLACK (#000000, #111111)
- Meaning: Power, sophistication, exclusivity
- Best for: Fashion, tech, luxury, professional services
- Pair with: Gold, white, red

GREEN (#2D6A4F, #40916C)
- Meaning: Growth, health, nature, trust, money
- Best for: Agriculture, health, finance, natural products
- Ghanaian context: Connected to the flag — evokes national pride

RED (#C1121F, #E63946)
- Meaning: Energy, urgency, passion, power
- Best for: Food, sales, sports, entertainment
- Use carefully: Can mean danger — balance with neutral tones

BLUE (#023E8A, #0077B6)
- Meaning: Trust, professionalism, calm, reliability
- Best for: Banks, tech, healthcare, law firms
- Most trusted color in business worldwide

WHITE (#FFFFFF, #F8F9FA)
- Meaning: Clean, pure, modern, premium
- Best for: Background, minimal design, health/beauty

SECTION 3 — PRACTICAL RULES
==========================================

THE 60-30-10 RULE
60% — Dominant color (usually background)
30% — Secondary color (sections, cards)
10% — Accent color (buttons, highlights)

CONTRAST IS KING
- Dark text on light background = readable
- Light text on dark background = readable
- Never: Light grey on white, yellow on white

FONTS + COLORS
- Bold font + simple color = professional
- Script font + warm color = friendly/creative
- Sans-serif + black/gold = modern luxury

==========================================
SECTION 4 — HOW TO USE YOUR COLORS
==========================================

LOGO: Use 1-2 brand colors max
WEBSITE HEADER: Strong, bold brand color
BUTTONS: Accent color — make them stand out
BACKGROUND: Light or neutral — let content breathe
HEADINGS: Dark color or brand color
BODY TEXT: Dark grey #333 or black — never full bright color
