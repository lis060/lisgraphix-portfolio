SEO QUICK WINS FOR LOCAL GHANAIAN BUSINESSES

10 actions you can do TODAY to get found on Google
=============================================

WHAT IS SEO?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) = making your business
appear on Google when people search for what you offer.
Example: Someone types "barbershop in Accra" — you want
YOUR business to show up, not your competitor.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #1 — CLAIM YOUR GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE
=============================================
This is the MOST important free thing you can do.
-> Go to: business.google.com
-> Search your business name
-> Claim and verify it
-> Add: photos, hours, phone, website, services
-> Ask 5 clients to leave a Google review

Result: Appear in Google Maps + local search results

=============================================
QUICK WIN #2 — PUT YOUR CITY IN YOUR PAGE TITLES
=============================================
Bad title:  "Home | My Business"
Good title: "Barbershop in Accra | Sharp Cuts"

Do this for every page on your website.
Tools: Your website builder (WordPress, Wix) or ask your developer.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #3 — ADD YOUR BUSINESS TO DIRECTORIES
=============================================
List your business on:
- Google Business Profile (business.google.com)
- Facebook Business Page
- Yellow Pages Ghana (yellowpagesghana.com)
- Jumia (if selling products)
- Instagram Business Account

Keep your Name, Address, Phone IDENTICAL on all platforms.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #4 — WRITE ONE HELPFUL BLOG POST
=============================================
Write a 500-word article answering a question your
customers always ask.

Examples:
- "How much does a website cost in Ghana?"
- "Best barbershops in East Legon 2025"
- "How to choose a real estate agent in Accra"

Post it on your website. Google loves helpful content.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #5 — GET 5 GOOGLE REVIEWS THIS WEEK
=============================================
Reviews are GOLD for local SEO.

How to ask:
"Hi [Name], I'm glad you enjoyed our service! 
Could you take 2 minutes to leave us a Google review?
Here's the link: [your Google Business link]"

Send this via WhatsApp to your 5 best clients TODAY.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #6 — COMPRESS ALL YOUR WEBSITE IMAGES
=============================================
Slow websites rank lower on Google.
Large images = slow website.

Free tool: squoosh.app
- Upload your image
- Set quality to 75-80%
- Download and replace on your website

Target: Every image under 200KB.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #7 — ADD LOCATION TO YOUR ABOUT PAGE
=============================================
Write naturally:
"We are a web design agency based in Accra, Ghana,
serving businesses across East Legon, Cantonments,
Airport Residential, and beyond."

Google uses this text to rank you locally.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #8 — CONNECT GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE
=============================================
Free tool from Google that shows:
- Which searches bring people to your site
- Which pages Google has indexed
- Any errors on your website

Setup: search.google.com/search-console
Add your website, verify ownership, submit sitemap.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #9 — ADD ALT TEXT TO ALL IMAGES
=============================================
Every image on your website should have a description.

Bad:  image123.jpg
Good: "professional-barbershop-accra-east-legon.jpg"
      Alt text: "Professional barbershop in East Legon, Accra"

This helps Google understand your images and ranks you
for image search too.

=============================================
QUICK WIN #10 — SHARE YOUR WEBSITE EVERY WEEK
=============================================
Post your website link on:
- WhatsApp status (every Monday)
- Instagram bio and every post caption
- Facebook page posts
- TikTok video descriptions

Each link back to your site tells Google you're real and active.

=============================================
BONUS TIP — BE PATIENT
=============================================
SEO takes 2-4 months to show results.
Do these 10 things consistently and you WILL rank.
The businesses that rank on page 1 started months ago.
Start today — your future self will thank you.
